Reaching us by bus:

From Hyderabad MGBS Bus Stand & Secundrabad Jubliee Bus Stand. There are Number of buses going to Karimnagar.
Book your tickets here


By car:

You can drive through NH163 Via Husnabad and drive to Karimnagar or Take a road via Siddhipet and Komuravelli. The temple is about 160 KM distance from Hyderabad.


By train:

From Mumbai or Secunderabad you should arrive in Nizamabad. It is the nearest Railway Station to Karimnagar. From Hyderabad 07793 Kacheguda - Peddapalli Demu express goes to Karimnagar.

By air:

Hyderabad International airport is 209 km away from ISKCON Karimnagar. There are direct busses available right from airport to Karimnagar.
