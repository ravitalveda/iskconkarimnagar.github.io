Welcome to Sri Sri Radha Govinda Mandir

"Vrindavan consciousness is having the spirit of loving service to Krsna wherever we are"

With the divine motto of spreading Vrindavan consciousness by introducing Krsna into your lives, which you will carry wherever you go.

Away from the bustling streets of Karimnagar, on the banks of river Godavari, on Vemulawada bypass, Ujjwala park road, opp Police training centre, extending over a 3 acre panoramic area, standing tall in its freshness & openness, the picturesque Abode of Sri Sri Radha Govinda, welcomes you to savour your soul, enjoy its serenity and improve the quality of your Life.
